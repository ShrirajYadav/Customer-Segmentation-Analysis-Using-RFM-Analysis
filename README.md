# 🛍️ Customer Segmentation Analysis Using RFM Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Analyzed customer purchasing behavior of an online retail company using **Python** and performed **RFM-based customer segmentation** to identify high-value, loyal, at-risk, and lost customers.

Generated actionable business insights and marketing recommendations to improve **customer retention** and **maximize revenue**.

---

## 🧩 Business Problem

An online retail company wanted to:
- Understand customer purchasing behavior at a deeper level
- Identify distinct customer segments
- Design **targeted marketing and retention strategies** for each segment

## 📊 Key Findings

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| 💰 Total Revenue Generated | £8.89 Million |
| 📦 Total Orders | 18,536 |
| 👥 Total Customers Analyzed | 4,339 |
| 🌍 Top Revenue Country | United Kingdom |

### 🏷️ Customer Segments Identified

| Segment | Key Insight |
|---------|------------|
| 🏆 Champions | Highest revenue contributors |
| 💛 Loyal Customers | Consistent buyers, strong upsell potential |
| ⚠️ At-Risk Customers | Previously active, showing drop-off |
| ❌ Lost Customers | **32.6%** of customer base — largest segment |

> **Notable Finding:** Customer spending was **highly concentrated** among a small group of high-value customers — a classic Pareto distribution pattern.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **🏆 Champions** → Retain through **exclusive loyalty programs and early access offers**
2. **💛 Loyal Customers** → **Upsell and cross-sell** premium products to increase order value
3. **⚠️ At-Risk Customers** → **Re-engage** with personalized discount campaigns and outreach
4. **❌ Lost Customers** → Launch **win-back campaigns** with strong incentives to recover churned revenue
